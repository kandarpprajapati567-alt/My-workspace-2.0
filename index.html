🕯️ MASTER CANDLESTICK PATTERNS

Your built-in educational folder to read market psychology.

1. Bullish Hammer (हथौड़ा)

Forms at the bottom of a downtrend. It shows sellers pushed the price down, but strong buyers rejected the low and pushed the price back up. Wait for the next candle to confirm a bullish reversal before entering.


2. Bearish Engulfing

Forms at the top of an uptrend. A small green candle is completely 'engulfed' or swallowed by a massive red candle. This indicates that sellers have violently taken control of the market.


3. The Doji (Indecision)

Looks like a cross or a plus sign. The open and close prices are exactly the same. It means a brutal war between buyers and sellers resulted in a tie. Often signals a major trend reversal is coming.


4. Morning Star Reversal

A powerful 3-candle pattern. 1) A strong red candle. 2) A small gap-down Doji (indecision). 3) A massive green candle pushing up. This is your green light for a trend flip!
